# Abstract

CrackDN Object Detection is a curated packaging of the CrackDataset_DL_HY box-level crack dataset for reproducible benchmarking in complex pavement backgrounds.

The associated research introduces CrackDN and evaluates sealed and unsealed crack detection under challenging conditions including unbalanced illumination, pavement markings, and shading.

This package preserves source publication metadata and adds standardized statistics and visualization artifacts for the Large Road Damage DataLake.
